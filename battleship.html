Battleship Antarctica

Battleship Antarctica is a 2D arcade-style shooter game developed in Java. You control a battleship fighting other ships amongst the icecaps of Antarctica. The game was developed as part of a team project in my second year of university. It features a custom built rendering and physics engine, and supports singleplayer and multiplayer modes.
