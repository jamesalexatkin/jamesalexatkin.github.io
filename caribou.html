Caribou Coffee Stores

This project allows for the visualisation of the number of Caribou Coffee stores in the United States by states and counties. The resulting visualisation is in the form of a choropleth, or shaded map. Darker regions represent a higher number of coffee stores. The maps are produced by folium, a Python library capable of rendering maps in HTML files.
